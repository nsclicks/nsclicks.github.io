About Me

It is said that if you are passionate about something, make it your hobby.

Hi, I’m Nick and I am a hobby photographer. I have been doing the professional photography for about a year now. I started doing the photography using my friend’s DLSR and initially I was shooting landscape. After reaching out to several people on different social media platforms about a fashion shoot, due to lack of the experience, I didn’t get much attention. That’s when I decided to use one of my friends as a guinea pig and did a shoot with her. Soon after I posted those pictures, I was able to schedule another fashion shoot with someone with a good amount of followers. After that shoot, I never looked back and continued growing my skills and my works started reaching out to more and more people every day. Within first six months, I had two magazine publications and one fashion show shoot on my photography resume. Very recently, I got published into another magazine making it 3 publications so far. I have done fashion, editorial, boudoir, family, event and couple shoots in my one year span. I am very lucky to marry a girl I love, who is very supportive of me and my passion about photography (most of the credit behind this website goes to her).

With this website, I am hoping that stories I put on the website reach out to people. They read the stories, get inspired by them, relate to them, and come out to share their own stories.
